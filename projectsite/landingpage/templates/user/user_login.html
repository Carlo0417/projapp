PSU Web-based Dormitory Information Management Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use and interact on our website (mobile and desktop). This is not a consent form but a general information on how PSU Dormitory processes personal data. By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy below.


Information We Collect

We collect Personal Data that you provide upon registration to the front desk officer of the dormitory which are under categories:

Personal Data, which includes your last name, first name, middle name, gender, address, city, municipality, province, and country;

University Data, which includes your enrolled program, and its office/department;

Contact Data, which includes your email address and contact number;

Transaction Data, which includes your payment details such as service name, payment amount, payment date, and official receipt number.


Personal Data may be collected as;

Last name, first name, and middle name, which are used for the management to identify the occupant who’ll be using the web application to prevent unauthorized access.

Gender, which is to identify the student’s gender, since the dormitories are divided into male and female dormitory. As stated in the Magna Carta of Women (Republic Act No. 9710) Gender and Development Program (GAD) where making women’s as well as men’s concerns and experiences an integral dimension of the design, implementation, monitoring, and evaluation of policies, programs and projects in all social, political, civil, and economic spheres so that women and men benefit equally. The collection of this sensitive data will also be used in academic research purposes and to generate reports for the dormitory.

Boarder type, which will be selected from the registration page to identify whether the occupant is local which resides here in the country or foreign which resides outside the country and will study and stay in Palawan.

Address, City, and Municipality, which are used for academic research purposes.

Province, which is the student’s origin since the university accommodates students outside Palawan.

Country, which is collected to identify student’s origin since the university accommodates exchange and foreign students. Also, it will be used for academic research purposes.

Enrolled program and its office/department, which will be used for academic purposes and to generate reports in the dormitory.

Email address and contact number, which will be used to contact the occupants and to send their statement of account as well as other notices.

Guardian’s first name and last name, which will be used to identify the occupant’s guardian to be informed for verification and emergency situations.

Guardian’s address, which will be used to locate them in time of emergency regarding the occupant which is related to them.

Guardian’s email address and contact number, which will be used to contact the guardian of an occupant and notify them whenever there is an emergency.

Lastly, payment details such as service name, payment amount and date, and official receipt number, which will be used to generate financial reports and are needed for the creation of statement of account.


How We Collect Your Information

We collect your data once you register to our system, directly from you with the front desk officer. We collect your data when you:

register to the system, directly with the front desk officer.

availed a service, which includes your payment details

contact us, when you inquire about the dormitory


How We Use Your Information

We may use your Personal Data for the following purposes:

To register you as an occupant.

To verify your identity and admission.

To communicate and notify you about your upcoming bills.

To manage our relationship with you.

To generate reports and track occupants.

To contact you in relation to all of the above.


Admission Requirements

The following are the documents needed for the admission requirements which are required by the University such as (2 pieces 2x2 colored ID pictures, Medical Certificate from the University physician, Fully Accomplished application form (form 01A-01D), Special power of attorney (SPA) for guardian, Photocopy of the University Identification card valid on the school year enrolled, Certificate of Enrollment, and Photocopy of the dormitory ID). We will use these documents for us to verify if you completed it for it was required and will be kept by the dormitory.

We will not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Whenever we are required by law or legislation to use your personal data for another purpose, such as when we must do so to abide by the relevant regulations and laws or to respond to administrative, judicial, or law enforcement requirements.


How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. To ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of personal data that is collected; organizational, administrative, and technical processes and procedures are put in place to safeguard the collected information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction.

The web application is built using Django as a framework of python, which are using encryption and hashing. By default, Django uses the PBKDF2 algorithm with a SHA256 hash, a password stretching mechanism recommended by NIST. Which is sufficient for most users, it’s quite secure, requiring massive amounts of computing time to break. By the use of encryption and hashing, it can protect sensitive data. Data is mostly stored in the database in human-readable format which is usually encrypted it python package django-cryptography.


Your Rights

You are afforded the following rights as written in RA 10173 otherwise known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

right to be informed whether personal information pertaining to him or her shall be, are being or have been processed.

right to reasonable access to the data subject’s information upon demand.

right to dispute the inaccuracy or error in the personal information and have the personal information controller correct it immediately and accordingly, unless the request is vexatious or otherwise unreasonable.

right to suspend, withdraw or order the blocking, removal or destruction of his or her personal information from the personal information controller’s filing system upon discovery and substantial proof that the personal information are incomplete, outdated, false, unlawfully obtained, used for unauthorized purposes or are no longer necessary for the purposes for which they were collected.

right to data portability thru the ability to obtain from the personal information controller a copy of data undergoing processing in an electronic or structured format, which is commonly used and allows for further use by the data subject.

right to lodge a complaint before the National Privacy Commission.


You have the right to access your personal information which can be done by logging into your account. Moreover, you can edit your credentials for security but are limited to:

password, which enables you to access your account.

security question and answer, which can be used for authentication.


Disclosure of Information

We may share your Personal Data when doing the following:

to the administrators for them to handle the information of the occupant in the system.

registration of data and admission requirements.

dealing with money-related or financial transactions such as handling services and generating statements of account.

oversee and monitor information and reports.

overall access, view, edit, correct, and modify information of the occupants.

We do not disclose your data and it is kept from unauthorized persons. Any unauthorized disclosure of your data will undergo legal action.


Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website and will be effective immediately upon posting. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically so that you are up to date on our most current policies and practices.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at psu.dormitory@psu.palawan.edu.ph
