Dormitory Rules and Regulations

These rules and regulations are not meant to deprive your freedom in the dormitory but for your protection as its resident. The following are the should do (do's) and the shouldn't (don'ts) of the PSU Dormitory:

SHOULD DO (DO's)

  • Residents must register/ log in the logbook every time they arrive and leave the dormitory premises.

  • Ask permission before doing anything to the dorm parents for approval.

  • All residents must be inside their respective rooms by 11:00 p.m. except when they need to use the study hall which is always open.

  • Residents who wish to leave the dormitory beyond the curfew hours must seek permission from dorm supervisor and secure a Curfew Extension Form. Complete all information in the Curfew Extension Form always. Abuse and failure to do so will result in demerit points. (See. Sec. 13 Guidelines of Curfew Extension Form (CEF))

  • ln case of lost key, the resident must report the incident to the Dorm parents within 24 hours. Resident should pay P200.OO for replacement.

  • Every dormitory room has one key for its occupants. Room key should be surrendered to the dormitory parent every time they will leave the dormitory premises.

  • Have their cell phone in case of emergency and should inform the dorm parents about their mobile number.

  • Visitors are not allowed inside the room, except resident's parents and siblings.

  • Visitors shall be entertained at the lounge area until 8:00 pm (weekdays) and 9:00 pm (weekends and holidays) only.

  • Observe curfew hours to avoid demerit points.

  • Sleep in his/her assigned room.

  • Always obey the dorm parents.

  • Respect one's privacy.

  • Write their names on all their personal belongings.

  • Always observe silence.

  • Always expected to follow the dormitory rules and dormitory obligations.

  • Can use the study hall only for reviewing and studying purposes only. Provided, that the resident shall leave the hall always clean.

  • Attend meetings and gatherings of the dormitory.

  • Keep their money and valuable things properly. The management will not be held liable for any loss of things/items.

  • Keep the room clean and all must be in a proper place.

  • Waste segregation is strictly implemented.

  • Clean the dining table anytime they use it and put all the utensils they used in the kitchen's sink.

  • Turn off the lights and fans when not in use.

  • Turn off gadgets such as laptop when not in use.

  • Conserve water and electricity as possible.

  • During emergency, residents must immediately inform the dorm parents or staff.

SHOULD NOT DO (DON'TS)

  • Not logging in and out on the logbook.

  • BOYS are not allowed to enter the Girls dormitory and Vice Versa.

  • Disturb others during the study time.

  • Play loud music and make noise at any time.

  • Going out during lights off (11:00 pm), except for an emergency case.

  • Sleep over with other residents other than their rooms mates is strictly prohibited.

  • Walking around beyond 11:00 pm outside dormitory boundaries.

  • Not turning off the appliances when not in use.

  • Allowing visitors to stay overnight in the dormitory.

  • Allowing outsider to enter the room of the residents.

  • Displaying disorderly conduct

  • Laundering of clothes inside the dormitory.

  • Cooking inside the room.

  • Use of electrical appliances, including ironing of clothes inside the dormitory premises.

  • Littering.

  • Noncompliance of waste segregation.

  • Vandalism.

  • Bullying.

  • Staying at friend's home or relatives during weekends should inform the dorm parents and is therefore subject for approval.

  • Spreading rumors that damaging the reputation of other residents.

  • Switching of rooms are strictly prohibited. And should not do it without permission from the dorm parents.

  • Do not move out room facilities without permission from the dorm parents.

  • Complaints about noisy individuals, destruction of dormitory property and violation of dormitory rules from dorm parents and dormitory staff will result in demerit points.

  • Stealing or attempting to steal money and other property.

  • Breaking rooms.

  • Any forms of gambling that involves money are strictly prohibited.

  • Not bring pets inside the dormitory.

  • Possession and bringing in of prohibited drugs and dangerous stuff.

  • Drinking alcohol and smoking are strictly prohibited.

  • Not quarrel, fight or be violent in any manner with other residents and dormitory officials.

  • Threatening and any attempt to any member of the dormitory with physical harm; unlawfully preventing or threatening the dormitory residents or other dormitory officials.

  • Giving or lending dormitory ID to an outsider and co-residents to enter the dormitory premises and avail the privileges.
